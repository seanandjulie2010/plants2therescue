Creamy Carrot & Red Lentil Soup

Vegan • Oil-Free • Sugar-Free • Nut-Free

A warm, comforting soup made with just a few pantry staples: red lentils, carrots, onion and simple spices.

Ingredients (3–4 servings)

  • 1 cup dry red lentils, rinsed
  • 3 large carrots, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth or water
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Freshly ground black pepper (optional)
  • Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add ingredients: In a pot, combine lentils, carrots, onion and broth or water.
  2. Cook: Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes, until lentils and carrots are very soft.
  3. Season: Stir in cumin, turmeric, garlic powder and pepper.
  4. Blend: Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth, or leave some texture if you prefer. Add a splash of water if you’d like a thinner consistency.
  5. Serve: Taste and adjust seasonings. Ladle into bowls and serve with a squeeze of lemon if desired.

Tips

  • Red lentils cook quickly and break down, making the soup naturally creamy.
  • Leftovers will thicken in the fridge; thin with a little water when reheating.
  • Add a pinch of smoked paprika for a deeper, smoky flavor if you like.
