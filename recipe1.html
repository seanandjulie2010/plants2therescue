Rainbow Avocado Chickpea Power Bowl

Vegan • Oil-Free • Sugar-Free • Nut-Free

This bowl is all about color, crunch and creaminess—whole-food fats from avocado, protein-rich chickpeas, and lots of fresh vegetables. Perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients (1–2 servings)

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (rinsed and drained if canned, no added salt if possible)
  • 1 small ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley or cilantro (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lime or lemon
  • 1–2 tablespoons water (to thin the dressing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional)
  • Salt-free seasoning blend or a pinch of salt if you use it

Instructions

  1. Prepare the base: Add the cooked brown rice or quinoa to the bottom of a bowl.
  2. Add the veggies and chickpeas: Arrange the chickpeas, red cabbage, carrot, cucumber and cherry tomatoes on top of the grain base.
  3. Make the avocado dressing: In a small bowl, mash the avocado with a fork. Stir in the lime or lemon juice, garlic powder, smoked paprika and 1–2 tablespoons of water until you have a pourable, creamy dressing. Adjust seasonings to taste.
  4. Assemble: Drizzle the avocado dressing over the bowl. Sprinkle with the chopped herbs and salt-free seasoning blend.
  5. Serve: Toss gently so everything gets coated and enjoy right away.

Tips

  • Use frozen, thawed veggies if fresh produce is limited—make it work with what you have.
  • For extra crunch, add shredded lettuce or finely chopped kale.
  • Make it a meal prep: keep dressing separate until serving so the veggies stay crisp.
