Cranberry Covenant Cake

Vegan • Oil-Free • Sugar-Free • Nut-Free

A tart-sweet, whole-food vegan cake—bright cranberries, orange warmth, and fruit-sweetened goodness that deepens as it rests.

Ingredients

Dry

  • 1½ cups whole wheat pastry flour (or spelt for a softer crumb)
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger (optional but beautiful with cranberry)
  • ¼ tsp salt

Wet

  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • ½ cup mashed ripe banana (very ripe = better sweetness)
  • ⅓ cup date paste (see note below)
  • ½ cup fresh orange juice (or apple juice)
  • Zest of 1 orange (optional but elevates everything)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Star Ingredient

  • 1½ cups fresh or frozen cranberries (If frozen, do not thaw)

Date Paste (Sugar-Free Sweetener)

  • ¾ cup pitted dates
  • ¾ cup hot water

Blend until smooth and thick. Measure after blending.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8 pan or loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk all wet ingredients until smooth and unified.
  4. Fold wet into dry gently — do not overmix.
  5. Toss cranberries with 1 tbsp flour, then fold them in last (keeps them suspended).
  6. Pour batter into pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 40–50 minutes, until a toothpick comes out mostly clean and the top springs back.
  8. Cool completely before slicing — this cake sets as it rests.

Warm together:

  • ½ cup cranberries
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 1 tbsp date paste
