Cranberry Covenant Cake

A tart-sweet, whole-food vegan cake that honors simplicity, patience, and the beauty of natural sweetness. Oil-free, sugar-free, nut-free.

Ingredients

Dry

  • 1½ cups whole wheat pastry flour (or spelt for a softer crumb)
  • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Wet

  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • ½ cup mashed ripe banana (very ripe preferred)
  • ⅓ cup date paste (see below)
  • ½ cup fresh orange juice (or apple juice)
  • Zest of 1 orange (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Star Ingredient

  • 1½ cups fresh or frozen cranberries (do not thaw if frozen)

Date Paste (Sugar-Free Sweetener)

  • ¾ cup pitted dates
  • ¾ cup hot water

Blend dates and water until smooth and thick. Measure after blending.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8×8 pan or loaf pan with parchment.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk all dry ingredients together.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk all wet ingredients until smooth and unified.
  4. Gently fold wet ingredients into dry. Do not overmix.
  5. Toss cranberries with 1 tablespoon flour, then fold them in last.
  6. Pour batter into pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 40–50 minutes, until a toothpick comes out mostly clean and the top springs back.
  8. Cool completely before slicing — this cake sets as it rests.

Optional Cranberry Crown (No Sugar Glaze)

  • ½ cup cranberries
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon date paste

Simmer ingredients together for 5–7 minutes until jammy. Spoon over the cooled cake before serving.
