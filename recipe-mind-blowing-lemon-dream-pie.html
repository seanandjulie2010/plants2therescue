Step 1: Soak the cashews

Place the raw cashews in a bowl and cover with plenty of water. Soak for 4‚Äì6 hours or overnight. This softens them so your filling blends silky smooth.

Step 2: Prepare the crust

  1. Add walnuts (or almonds), dates, sea salt, and lemon zest to a food processor.
  2. Pulse until crumbly but sticky ‚Äî it should hold together when pinched.
  3. Line a 9-inch pie dish with parchment paper.
  4. Press the crust firmly into the bottom (and slightly up the sides).

Step 3: Blend the lemon filling

  1. Drain the soaked cashews (and dates if you soaked them).
  2. Add all filling ingredients to a high-speed blender.
  3. Blend for 2‚Äì3 minutes, stopping to scrape down the sides as needed.

Step 5: Chill and set

Refrigerate the pie for at least 6 hours, but overnight is best. This is what helps it firm up and slice cleanly.

Step 6: Serve

Let the pie sit at room temperature for 10 minutes before slicing for the creamiest texture. Top with coconut whipped cream, berries, and extra lemon zest, if desired.
