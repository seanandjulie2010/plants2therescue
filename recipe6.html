Simple Baked Potato & Greens Plate

Vegan • Oil-Free • Sugar-Free • Nut-Free

A minimalist “reset” meal: a fluffy baked potato and tender seasoned greens. Easy, grounding and budget-friendly.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 large russet or sweet potato
  • 2 cups chopped kale, collards or spinach
  • 2–3 tablespoons water or vegetable broth (for sautéing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt-free seasoning blend or a pinch of salt if you use it
  • Lemon wedge for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bake the potato: Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Pierce the potato a few times with a fork and bake for 45–50 minutes, until tender. For a faster version, microwave for 7–8 minutes, turning halfway through.
