Lemon Cloud Cups

Vegan • Oil-Free • Sugar-Free • Nut-Free • Whole-Food

Why it’s mind-blowing

No sugar. No oil. No nuts. The creaminess comes from oats, the sweetness from fruit, and the punch from real lemon.

Ingredients (2–3 servings)

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1½ cups water
  • Zest of 1 large lemon
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ripe banana (very ripe = better sweetness)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of turmeric (optional, for color only)
  • Pinch of salt (optional but enhances lemon)

Instructions

  1. Cook the base: Simmer oats + water 8–10 minutes until very soft.
  2. Blend: Add banana, lemon juice, zest, vanilla (and turmeric). Blend until silky.
  3. Chill: Pour into jars/cups. Refrigerate at least 1 hour (2 hours is best).
  4. Serve: Cold, creamy, and bright.

Optional toppings (still compliant)

  • Extra lemon zest
  • Fresh berries
  • Thin curls of lemon peel for presentation
