Vegan Matcha Green Tea Pudding

Vegan • Oil-Free • No Refined Sugar • Whole-Food Plant-Based

Ingredients

  • 1 can (13.5 oz) unsweetened light coconut milk
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons matcha green tea powder
  • 4–6 Medjool dates, blended into a paste
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch or arrowroot
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend dates with a splash of almond milk into a smooth paste.
  2. Whisk matcha with warm water to remove clumps.
  3. Combine coconut milk, almond milk, matcha, date paste, vanilla, and salt in a pot.
  4. Mix cornstarch with 1/4 cup water; stir into warm mixture.
  5. Heat 3–5 minutes until thick and silky (avoid boiling hard).
  6. Pour into bowls and chill 2 hours before serving.
