Why this works

  • Matcha delivers calm energy + antioxidants.

  • Coconut + almond milk make it silky without oil.
  • Cornstarch thickens gently for a custard-like texture.

Ingredients

Instructions

  4. Mix in matcha. Whisk in the matcha paste until the pudding is a consistent soft green color.
