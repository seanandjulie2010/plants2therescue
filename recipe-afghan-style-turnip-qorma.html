Why this works

Turnips soften and mellow as they simmer, absorbing the warmth of Afghan spices and the richness of slow-cooked tomatoes. Caramelized onions bring natural sweetness without added sugar, proving that deeply comforting food needs neither oil nor excess to nourish body and spirit.
