Why this works

Blood oranges naturally caramelize as they bake, creating a rich, jewel-toned topping without oil or refined sugar. Applesauce and whole-food sweetness keep the crumb moist and tender, while citrus zest brings brightness and depth. This cake feels indulgent while staying rooted in simple, nourishing ingredients.
