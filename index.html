Our Mission

At Plants 2 the Rescue we believe long-term weight loss is about more than calorie counting — it’s about nourishing the mind, body and spirit while addressing the underlying causes of overeating. Our program centres around a vibrant, whole-food plant-based diet that fuels your body with nutrient-dense foods, calms inflammation and supports sustainable change.

We combine vegan nutrition education with trauma-informed coaching, mindful movement and spiritual practices to help you heal the root of why food became a coping mechanism. By integrating emotional awareness, compassionate self-care and community support, we empower you to break free from patterns of overeating and create a life of balance, health and joy.

We are currently only offering limited coaching with very few time slots available. Please contact us to check availability, and thank you for your understanding.

How It Works

  1. Start simple: build meals around whole, plant foods.
  2. Heal deeper: learn tools for stress, cravings, and emotional eating.
  3. Stay supported: community + optional coaching to keep momentum.
