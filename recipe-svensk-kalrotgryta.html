Why this works

Rutabaga has long been a staple in Scandinavian kitchens, offering natural sweetness and heartiness without the need for added sugar or fat. White beans provide creaminess and protein, while dill and allspice gently echo traditional Swedish flavors. Simple ingredients, slow cooking, and nothing hidden.
