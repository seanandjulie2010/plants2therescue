Lemon Herb Quinoa Bowl

Vegan • Oil-Free • Sugar-Free • Nut-Free

A bright, minimalistic bowl made with simple whole-food ingredients: quinoa, fresh vegetables, and a light lemon-herb dressing.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 cup dry quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 cup baby spinach or mixed greens
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley or cilantro (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Freshly ground black pepper (optional)
  • Salt-free seasoning blend or a pinch of salt if you use it

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa: Add rinsed quinoa and water to a pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Turn off the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  2. Prep the vegetables: While the quinoa cooks, chop the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and greens.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, garlic powder, oregano, pepper and seasoning.
  4. Assemble the bowls: Divide quinoa between two bowls. Top with tomatoes, cucumber, spinach and fresh herbs.
  5. Dress and serve: Drizzle the lemon dressing over each bowl, toss gently if desired, and enjoy.

Tips

  • Use leftover cooked quinoa from the fridge to make this even faster.
  • Add canned chickpeas (rinsed) for extra protein if you like.
  • This is delicious served warm or chilled as a make-ahead lunch.
