These baked oat cups are like portable apple cinnamon oatmeal‚Äînaturally sweetened with fruit, perfect for breakfast or a snack.

Ingredients (10‚Äì12 cups)

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 cup unsweetened soy, oat or other nut-free plant milk
  • 1 medium apple, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup raisins or chopped dates (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven and prepare muffin tin.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients.
  4. Fold in apple and optional fruit.
  5. Fill muffin cups.
  6. Bake until set and lightly golden.
  7. Cool briefly and serve.

Tips

  • Reheat with a splash of plant milk if desired.
  • Add extra cinnamon for more spice.
  • Skip raisins/dates for very low sweetness.
